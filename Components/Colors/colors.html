Colors

Feather UI uses following colors for all its components.

#ff3f6c

primary

#ff723f

secondary

#f87171

danger

#fbbf24

warning

#60a5fa

info

#34d339

success

#f7f7f7

lynx-white

#c5c5c5

lynx-grey

#9a9a9a

grey

#282828

black

To use any of these colors as the CSS color property, just add the color-name-clr as a class.

You can also choose these as your background color by adding color-name-bg as the element class.
