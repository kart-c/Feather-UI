Rating

Ratings provide insight regarding others' opinions and experiences, and can allow the user to submit a rating of their own.

Simple Rating

Add the rating-container class to icons to create a rating container.

Rating Component Sizes

Use the md and lg classes to change the size of the rating container.

Rating Fill

To toggle the fill of the rating icon simply change the far class to fas class.
